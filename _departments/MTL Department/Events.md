## Events

The Mother Tongue Fortnight  aims to provide opportunities for students to develop greater interest in learning Mother Tongue Language and to understand their own culture. The department organized various activities for all levels of students such as cultural camp, traditional candy and dumpling making, drama challenge, language quiz and learning journeys  to enhance their interest and love for the language and culture.
