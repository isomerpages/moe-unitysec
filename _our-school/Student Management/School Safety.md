<h2>School Safety</h2>
<p>Road safety is the responsibility of all staff, parents and students.</p>
<p><strong>Students are to practise the following when crossing roads and entering into the school compound:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Always use pedestriansâ€™ traffic crossings such as traffic lights and zebra
crossing and strictly abide by traffic rules.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Do not cross when the 'green man' is flashing as you will not be able
to complete the crossing.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Do not rush and always look out for traffic in all directions (look right-left-right).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Do not stand in between parked vehicles or in front of stationary buses/big
vehicles as the drivers may not see you.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be considerate and cross the road as soon as you can safely.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Refrain from doing things that might distract you while crossing such
as using the phone.</p>
</li>

</ul>
<p><strong>Parents are to take note of the following when dropping off students in the school compound:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Strictly follow Safe Route to drop off your child as shown below.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Always keep a good lookout for students when driving into the school compound.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Keep to the 15 kmph speed limit while driving into the school compound.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Do not use handphone while driving.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Obey directional signs and follow lane markings that have been set up.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are to alight at the designated drop off point (in front of foyer)
and not anywhere else.</p>
</li>
<li>

the school compound.</p>
