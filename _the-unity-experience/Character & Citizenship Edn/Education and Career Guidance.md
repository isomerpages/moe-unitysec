<h2><strong>Education and Career Guidance (ECG)</strong></h2>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<h4><strong>At Unity Secondary School, we strive to:</strong></h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Provide accurate and comprehensive education and career information</strong> to
help students explore viable pathways for their future. <em>(Knowledge)</em>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Develop students’ self-awareness, self-management, and lifelong learning skills</strong> to
enable them to take ownership of their personal growth and career development. <em>(Skills)</em>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Foster an appreciation for the dignity and value of all occupations</strong> and
their collective contribution to the well-being of society. <em>(Mindset)</em>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Empower students with communication and collaboration skills</strong> to
engage their parents meaningfully in their decision-making journey. <em>(Engaging the Community)</em>
</p>
</li>
</ol>

<p><strong>ECG Outcomes for Secondary Students</strong>
</p>
<p>Our ECG programmes are planned and conducted for students at various levels
in alignment with the ECG Development Model by the Ministry of Education.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The goals of ECG are to support students in:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Discovering purpose</strong>&nbsp;- Who am I? Nurture students'
self-awareness to support them in discovering how they can meaningfully
play a part in their community.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Exploring opportunities</strong>&nbsp;- Where do I want to go?
Develop students' self-directedness and confidence to explore and leverage
education and career opportunities while respecting the value of all occupations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Staying relevant</strong>&nbsp;- How do I get there? Build students'
adaptability and resilience to embrace the need for lifelong learning.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<h4><strong>ECG Experiences @ Unity Secondary School</strong></h4>
<p><strong><br></strong>Through our ECG experiences, we aim to guide students
to:</p>

to make informed and confident decisions about their post-secondary education

<p><strong>Education and Career Guidance (ECG) Counsellor</strong>
<br>The school also has an in-house ECG Counsellor who provides the following
to students:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>up-to-date information and resources on post-secondary education</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>individual and/or group counselling and guidance sessions with the aim
of helping students make well-informed decisions or manage issues related
to education and career choices</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>work closely with the school to support the planning and delivery of ECG
strategies that are customised to the school’s needs, profile or areas
of priority</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Students may make appointments to meet up with the ECG Counsellor in school
through their Character Coaches or make an appointment by emailing the
ECG Counsellor at&nbsp;<a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">phoa_ang_hiok@schools.gov.sg</a>.</p>
