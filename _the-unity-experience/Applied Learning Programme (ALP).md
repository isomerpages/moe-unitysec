<h2>Applied Learning Programme (ALP)</h2>
<p>GreenovAtors In Action (GAIA): Think &amp; Tinker To Champion Sustainability.</p>
<p>Ever imagine what can be done to make Singapore’s food production more
sustainable? Ever wonder what an energy-efficient community looks like
in 2050? Spot Unity’s eco-innovators in action as they attempt to address
real-world challenges in sustainability with refreshing tech-enabled solutions!</p>
<p>GreenovAtors In Action (GAIA) is our STEM Applied Learning Programme (ALP).
It aims to equip our students with 21st Century Competencies, science concepts
and technological skills to understand real world challenges and propose
solutions to address these challenges.</p>
