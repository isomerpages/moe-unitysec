<h1>CCA Leaders</h1>
<h3>GENERAL ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITY OF CCA LEADERS</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To provide visionary leadership for the CCA in setting and achieving goals</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To lead and organize the CCA in the planning and conduct of meetings,
activities and programmes</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To promote teamwork and manage relationships in the CCA</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To liaise with the school and the CCA teachers through effective communication</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To assist the CCA teachers in the running of the CCA</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To mentor and develop members of the CCA</p>
</li>
</ul>

<h3>CCA LEADERS PRIDE</h3>
<p>We, the <strong>CCA Leaders</strong> of Unity Secondary School
<br>Promise to <strong>LEAD</strong>, <strong>SERVE</strong> and <strong>INSPIRE</strong> our
members
<br>We pledge to carry out our duties
<br>With <strong>COMMITMENT</strong>, <strong>INTEGRITY </strong>and <strong>COMPASSION </strong>
<br>We will <strong>RESPECT</strong> and acknowledge every effort put in
<br>By our members to excel in our CCAs
<br>We will persevere to achieve our desired goals
<br>And bring glory to our CCAs
<br>In Unity, <strong>WE EXCEL!</strong>
</p>
